About Valley Lodges

Nestled in the serene beauty of Hauna, Honde Valley, Valley Lodges was founded in 2017 by Zephaniah Marevangepo and his wife, Maggie Mwadiwa. What began as a humble dream has grown into one of Manicaland’s premier destinations for luxury accommodation, fine dining, and breathtaking experiences.

Our mission is to provide the best accommodation services in all of Manicaland and beyond — offering guests a perfect blend of comfort, tranquility, and unmatched hospitality.
